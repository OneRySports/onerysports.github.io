Bobby Portis to participate in three-point contest

Feb 09, 2026 • Mukwonago, WI
Greetings, sports fans! Let me tell you about the latest development that has everyone buzzing around Milwaukee - Bobby Portis is set to participate in this season’s three-point contest at All-Star Weekend. This is a big deal for Portis and the Bucks organization alike as it marks his first time ever taking part in an All-Star event during his 11-year career! Not only will Portis be representing the Bucks, but he's having one of his best seasons shooting from behind the arc, hitting 45.1% on 4.3 attempts per game. That mark puts him in a virtual three-way tie for fifth place in 3P% with Sam Merrill and Ayo Dosunmu - impressive numbers indeed! Even our old friend Damian Lillard is making his return to the event after not playing at all this season due to a torn Achilles. He's looking to add another three-point title to his extensive resume, having won back-to-back competitions in 2023 and 2024 (the latter while with the Bucks). So it looks like we'll have quite a showdown between these two great shooters! The NBA explained how the event works: "In the two-round contest, players attempt to score as many points as possible from multiple 3-point locations within 70 seconds. The top three scorers from the first round advance to the championship round, where the highest score determines the winner." We can expect some intense competition between Portis, Lillard and other talented shooters such as Donovan Mitchell, Tyrese Maxey, Jamal Murray, Norman Powell, Devin Booker, and Kon Knueppel. So mark your calendars for next Saturday at 4:00 p.m. Central time on NBC and Peacock - this is an event you won't want to miss!
