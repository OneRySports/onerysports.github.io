Green Bay Packers News: Now the offseason can well and truly begin

Feb 10 • Mukwonago, WI
Green Bay Packers News: Now the offseason can well and truly begin The Green Bay Packers’ offseason has officially kicked off with most of the coaching changes complete, but one big thing needed to happen before everything could be set in stone. That’s the Super Bowl, which wrapped up the season on Sunday night with a 29-13 Seattle Seahawks victory over the New England Patriots. All eyes now shift to next February as the race to Super Bowl LXI begins. The Seahawks went wire-to-wire as one of the NFL’s best teams and were easily one of the most complete as well. Sam Darnold is a Super Bowl-winning starting quarterback, but it was the Seahawks’ defense that really stole the show on Sunday evening as they never let Drake Maye and the Pats’ offense get into a rhythm. Now the Packers and the NFL as a whole shift their focus to preparing for next year’s Super Bowl, which is a little over a year away. Next year’s game will take place on Valentine’s Day – a perfect time for someone named Love to win a ring, wouldn’t you say? In the first curds of the true offseason, we find the new Packers president explaining why he extended the head coach, a look back at Packers Super Bowls past, and the start of some rumblings about free agency.
