CF Montreal acquire F Noah Streit from FC Basel 1893

Feb 09 • Mukwonago, WI
CF Montreal have acquired forward Noah Streite from Swiss side FC Basel 1893. The deal, which was announced on Tuesday by the Canadian Premier League club, will see Streite join up with his new teammates as they continue their preseason preparations ahead of the upcoming season. Streite, a versatile and energetic winger, has spent time at both youth and senior levels across Europe before making this move to North America. He brings experience playing in some of the top leagues on the continent and will be looking to make an impact with CF Montreal. The signing comes as good news for fans who were hoping that their team would bolster its attack following last season's disappointing finish. With several other transfers still being worked out, it seems like head coach Wilfried Nancy is committed to building a strong squad capable of challenging for silverware this year. In other football news, there has been speculation surrounding the future of midfielder Samuel Umtiti after he failed to reach an agreement with Barcelona over extending his contract. The Frenchman's agent recently stated that talks between both parties have broken down entirely, leaving many wondering what direction things may go next.
