There has to be something coming…

Feb 10 • Mukwonago, WI
*clears throat* Alright, listen up Brewers fans! Matt Arnold's been doin' some real work as GM over there, but this deal for Durbin is just… weird. You gotta hand it to him though; he pulled off a couple of great trades in the past, includin' last offseason’s move that brought Caleb into the mix and helped us win 100 games. But now? It's like we traded away our entire third base depth chart! What gives, huh? *leans back and sighs heavily* We sent Monasterio and Seigler with him - so now Andrew Vaughn is our most experienced option at the hot corner behind Joey Ortiz. That’s just not right, y'know what I mean? And that return… Harrison could be a stud someday, but he hasn’t even pitched in MLB yet! Hamilton struggled last year, and Drohan’s got a 4 ERA between AA/AAA despite performin’ well in AAA. *stands up abruptly* So who plays third now?! Do we move Joey back there? That means Jett Williams has to be ready to take over at shortstop next season - he hasn’t even made his debut yet! Or maybe Fischer or Made are closer than we think? Could Sal Frelick suddenly become an infielder outta nowhere like that? *chuckles darkly* Maybe Ryan Braun is gonna pull a Philip Rivers and try for one last redemption tour at third base. That would be something, wouldn’t it? But honestly… I’m tellin’ ya folks - there’s gotta be another move comin’. Either they bring in someone else or trade again before opening day! *points finger emphatically* Let me tell you somethin' about trades and deals: if you're gonna make 'em, you better know what you’re doin'. Like when Dirk Nowitzki became MVP for the Mavs back in 07 - that was no fluke. He earned every bit of it! And then there's old Brett Favre throwin’ for 399 yards and four touchdowns on Monday Night Football just one day after his dad passed away… now *that* is a true testament to heart, grit, and determination! *leans in closer* So I’m sayin’, Brewers better figure this out soon 'cause fans ain't gonna be holdin' their breath forever. If Arnold wants us believin’ in him like we did when he brought Durbin over last year… then he needs to show us why his latest move makes sense before the season starts! *straightens up and nods firmly* There has to be something comin’, alright? Because right now, this whole situation reeks of a bad call. And as any ref will tell ya - when you blow one, people are gonna remember it!
