2026 WR Fantasy Football Rankings, WR PPR Cheat Sheets, WR PPR Draft / Draft Rankings - FantasyPros

Feb 09 • Mukwonago, WI
Here are the top 5 wide receivers for fantasy football in 2026, based on their projected performance and value in PPR leagues: **1. Justin Jefferson, Minnesota Vikings** * **Projected Stats:** 87 Rec | 1,450 Yds | 12 TDs * **Why He's #1:** Jefferson’s elite route-running, hands, and consistency make him the gold standard at WR. Kirk Cousins' accuracy and the Vikings' offensive identity as a pass-first team ensure targets flow his way. His ability to win 50/50 balls makes him a must-start every week. **2. Tyreek Hill, Miami Dolphins** * **Projected Stats:** 98 Rec | 1,600 Yds | 14 TDs * **Why He's #2:** Hill’s speed and hands are unmatched. Tua Tagovailoa’s improved accuracy and the Dolphins' explosive offense create massive opportunities for deep shots. While targets may dip slightly with Tyreek Davis and Mike Gesicki, his efficiency on fewer snaps keeps him elite. **3. Garrett Wilson, New York Jets** * **Projected Stats:** 92 Rec | 1,400 Yds | 8 TDs * **Why He's #3:** The undisputed top target in a pass-happy Jets offense with Aaron Rodgers. His separation skills and improving contested-catch rate make him a weekly bell cow. Consistency is his calling card. **4. Davante Adams, Las Vegas Raiders** * **Projected Stats:** 85 Rec | 1,300 Yds | 9 TDs * **Why He's #4:** Still an elite route runner and hands catcher at age 32. Jimmy Garoppolo’s deep-ball accuracy complements his skillset perfectly in a high-volume Raiders passing game. **5. CeeDee Lamb, Dallas Cowboys** * **Projected Stats:** 89 Rec | 1,400 Yds | 10 TDs * **Why He's #5:** Dak Prescott’s trust and the Cowboys' offensive schemes create high-volume targets for Lamb. His ability to win on slants, comebacks, and intermediate routes makes him a WR2/WR3 floor with WR1 upside in PPR. **Key Notes & Trends:** * **PPR Impact:** Jefferson (high YPC), Hill (TD potential), Wilson (target volume) are premium PPR assets. Lamb’s high catch rate and Adams’ deep-ball efficiency also shine here. * **Top Targets Matter Most:** In today’s pass-heavy NFL, players like Wilson and Lamb who are the clear #1 in their respective rooms dominate rankings. * **QBs Drive WR Production:** Jefferson/Hill (Tua), Wilson (Rodgers), Adams (Jimmy G) have elite QBs throwing them the ball. **Early Draft Strategy:** Target Jefferson or Hill early, then add a high-volume target like Wilson or Lamb in rounds 2-4 for PPR leagues. Avoid overvaluing deep sleepers without proven quarterback play. As Oscar Robertson showed with the Bucks, having the right pieces around you (like a sharp QB) can elevate your game to championship level.
