But tonight, he proved all those doubters wrong and cemented his legacy as one of the best quarterbacks in the league today. It's moments like these that make you realize just how much heart and determination Darnold has inside him because anyone else might have thrown up their hands in frustration after facing so much adversity throughout his career thus far but not our boy from Texas A&M!
